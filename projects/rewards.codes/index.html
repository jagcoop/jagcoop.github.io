**Rewards Codes** Loyalty platform [Rewards Codes - RwC](https://rewards.codes) is a simple loyalty platform conceived for *small* companies to use **big** strategies for customers retention. This project started from a closed project "SigueMS" an SMS interface for twitter. 50% of the loyalty strategies fails mainly because are: 1. Hard to use 2. Hard to understand 3. Unappealing rewards Rewards codes uses best practices in retail and service industry to tackle these problems. Implements only two steps to make it easy to understand. Using worldwide communication platforms to make it easy to use and by exchanging rewards points inside our business network to always have a desirable reward. Thanks to our clients Rewards Codes became the tool to open positive communications between a business and its customers. Is fascinating how this communication started with one visit of the customer to a business and evolves to a service feedback, new purchases, launching new services or products, answering frequently questions efficiently and more...
