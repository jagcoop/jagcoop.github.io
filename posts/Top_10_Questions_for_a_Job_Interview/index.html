# Top 10 Common Questions for an Interview By Pepe Hernandez. December 11th, 2024 ## Moster Lists for top 10 questions Interviewers use common questions. Here is a list of 10 common questions and some tips for answering them (from an Article by Moster). Be prepared and practice. 1. **What are your weaknesses?** Concentrate on professional qualities and minimize your weaknesses 2. **Why should we hire you?** Summarize your experience. 3. **Why do you want to work here?** It looks for knowledge about the company. 4. **What are your goals?** Your immediate and medium term goals. 5. **Why did you leave, or why are you leaving) your job?** Give some context and answer. 6. What can you do for us that other candidates can't? Mention the valuable (someone can pay for that) or rare skills that differentiate you from the others. 7. When were you most satisfied with your job? Where you find motivation. 8. What would your last boss say three positive things about you? Brag in other people words. 9. What salary are your seeking? Research previously to be inside the range or say you would adapt to the wages if they can tell you the range. 10. If you were an animal, which one would you want to be? Do not think of passive or aggressive animals. Look for a good answer. The best way to answer a question is to be prepared for the response. Answer these questions, record yourself, and keep practicing. ## Don Georgovich list The following is a list of the top 10 common questions from Don's experience: 1. Tell me about yourself.
